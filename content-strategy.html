I manage MedSageHQ, a platform dedicated to promoting health education and delivering clear, accurate medical information. Through MedSageHQ, I create well-researched and informative content that translates complex medical terminology into language that is easy for the general public to understand. I also work closely with healthcare professionals to ensure that the content is reliable and value-driven. My goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge they need to make informed health decisions.

In addition to my work with MedSageHQ, I run DriveVirtual, a platform focused on intelligent and engaging automotive content. Despite not having physical access to the vehicles, I conduct extensive research and use data-driven insights to explore various aspects of car performance, design, and market trends. I present this information in a visually appealing and accessible way to help car enthusiasts and potential buyers better understand the vehicles they are interested in.

As a graphics designer, I bring a strong visual identity to both platforms. I create high-quality visuals that are clean, consistent with each brand, and strategically designed to enhance audience engagement. Whether it's an infographic, a digital brochure, or a social media post, every design element is crafted with purpose to support the message and improve the user experience.

Through these projects, I combine creativity, research, and communication to build platforms that inform, inspire, and connect with diverse audiences.
